Alpine Lakes on my Mind





When realizing that warm weather had started to transition over to the rainy season here in Northwest Oregon, I thought of all the alpine lakes I could explore next summer. One of the lakes I didn't get the chance to go to this year was Olallie Lake. I remembered camping there with my boyfriend and our neighbor last year to camp. I promised myself I would make it a point to go camping there again next summer. This image is inspired by the moment that I made that decision. I created this image using a double exposure technique in Adobe Photoshop CC.
