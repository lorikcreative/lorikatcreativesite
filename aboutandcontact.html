About Me

Hi, my name is Lori Kat Meier and I am a Information Technology student at Colorado Technical University Online, specializing in web design and development. I built this website as my Senior Design Project and as a way to market my creative work further. My main professional interests include front-end web development, motion design, illustration and animation. I am a very curious person and strive to learn more and more every day. Prior to my education at Colorado Technical University, I went to school for Visual Communications at ITT Tech, where I learned all about digital design and video production. I also do amateur photography, with many images used for my projects being self-taken. In my free time, I enjoy snowboarding, hiking, yoga, paddleboarding and going on scenic drives.
