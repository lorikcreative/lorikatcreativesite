Pacific Northwest Starry Kinetic Word Cloud





When I moved to Oregon in 2018, I started recreating in the outdoors immediately. I new that the Santiam Pass, Mount Hood and many trails and hot springs were amongst the many places to play that I had available to me. Part of how I recreate is remembering to be respectful of my surroundings and of the land. This has been true for years, but is even more important now that I live in one of the most beautiful places on Earth. The pride I take in recreating responsibly inspired this video, reminding visitors of the Pacific Northwest to leave no trace when recreating in the outdoors.
